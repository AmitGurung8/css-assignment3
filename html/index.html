Introduction

Snickers is a popular chocolate bar made by Mars, Incorporated. It consists of nougat, caramel, and peanuts covered in milk chocolate. Snickers is known for its satisfying combination of flavors and textures, making it a beloved treat worldwide.

Interesting Facts

Discover fascinating facts about Snickers candy. From its humble beginnings to its global presence, learn about the history, trivia, and fun facts that make Snickers a truly iconic treat.

  • Snickers was first introduced in 1930.
  • The bar was named after the Mars family's favorite horse.
  • Snickers is sold in over 70 countries worldwide.
